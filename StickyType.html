About

Welcome to Sticky Type, the ultimate destination for honing your typing skills! Whether you're a seasoned typist looking to improve your speed and accuracy or a beginner eager to embark on a typing journey, Sticky Type is here to empower you. Our interactive platform offers a comprehensive and enjoyable typing experience. With a sleek and user-friendly interface, Sticky Type provides a variety of engaging exercises and challenges designed to elevate your typing proficiency. From classic typing drills to fun and innovative games, we have something to cater to every skill level and interest.
